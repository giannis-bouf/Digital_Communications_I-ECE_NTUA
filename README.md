This repository contains the laboratory exercises and simulations developed for the **Digital Communications I** course at the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering, **National Technical University of Athens (NTUA)**, during the academic year 2022-2023.

---

## ðŸ“– Course Overview
The course covers the fundamental principles of digital signal transmission and reception. Key topics include:
* **Baseband Transmission:** PAM signaling, Pulse shaping (Nyquist criterion, Raised Cosine).
* **Stochastic Processes & Noise:** Analysis of AWGN channels and SNR impact.
* **Digital Modulations:** M-ary PAM, PSK, QAM, and FSK.
* **Performance Analysis:** Calculation and simulation of Bit Error Rate (BER) vs. $E_b/N_0$.
* **Optimal Receivers:** Matched Filter and Maximum Likelihood (ML) detection.
